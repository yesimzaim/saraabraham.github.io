"Whether you are doing something interesting or not, we would advise you to stop. Take a few seconds to breathe and dive into Big Man, the new song from 22-year-old singer Sara Abraham. Indeed, in a time when everything goes fast, when many don't take the time to appreciate beautiful things, allowing yourself this musical break is one of the best things you can do today. Thanks to a voice enrolled in a soft sensitivity that transports you, Sara Abraham leaves you no choice. She grabs you, captivates you, engages you in a delicate veil of beautiful things. Relentless melodies, a musicality that transpires from the first bars, a production of great quality in short, Sara Abraham delivers a little addiction to you, a song that we listen to again and again, that we never tire of."

- Review form Iggy Mag, 6 June 2020
